Entha Kathe Maaraya

Editor Kannada Film Social Drama Actor

Entha Kathe Maaraya is an Indian Kannada social drama film written and directed by Rakshith Thirthahalli. The film features Sudheer SJ (Editor & Actor), Vedhanth Subramanya, Sripriya, Ashwin Hasan, Keshav Guttalike, Sameer Nagarad, Raviraj Shetty, and Pallavi Gopal in leading roles.

View on IMDb

Plot

In a Karnataka village, engineer Sudheer struggles with his loyalty to the government when plans to divert water threaten locals' lives. Led by Dakanna, Sharavati, Bojanna, and villagers unite against the project, highlighting the strength of community in adversity.

Nestled in the idyllic countryside of Karnataka, the film delves into the life of Dakanna, a revered elder known for his unwavering principles and deep connection to the land. The tranquility of the village is shattered when Sudheer, a determined DPR engineer, unveils plans for a controversial river diversion project.
