Critical Acclaim

Entha Kathe Maaraaya resonates deeply with its poignant portrayal of environmental activism amidst Karnataka's water crisis. Through mesmerizing storytelling and evocative cinematography, it prompts viewers to reflect on the urgent need for sustainable solutions.

— IMDb User Review (10/10)

A riveting tale of environmental activism and community resilience. The film's captivating narrative weaves together the struggles of diverse characters against the backdrop of the revered Sharavathi River.

— Featured Review

Screened at the Chennai International Film Festival, this film highlights the ongoing water crisis and global warming issues in Bangalore with rare scenarios never seen in the Western Ghats.

— The New Indian Express

A cinematic masterpiece that leaves a lasting impression, urging audiences to embrace environmental stewardship and community resilience. Sudheer SJ's editing and VFX work is exceptional.

— Film Festival Review

The film delves into current environmental phenomena with a full-fledged entertaining narrative. Based on the story of Kadina Nentaru Forests which serve as the primary source of water.

— Kannada Cinema Review

With an IMDb rating of 9.4/10, Enthaa Kathe Maaraaya stands as a powerful testament to independent Kannada cinema's ability to tackle pressing social issues with artistic excellence.

— Cinema Enthusiast
